Bookdle: An Infinite "Guess That Book" game!

1. Recieve and read a 90% redacted summary of the book.

2. Make a guess of what the book is using only the incomplete summary (no google!).

3. If you're wrong, you'll recieve a new summary that is only 80% redacted.

4. When you have 7 tries remaining, you'll recieve a hint: the publication date of the book.

5. When you have 5 tries remaining, you'll recieve another hint: the genre/genres of the book.

6. When you have 2 tries remaining, you'll recieve your last hint: the author of the book.

Tip: When you think you know the answer, run it through the rectangular search bar on the bottom to make sure you spell it right.

You get 10 tries per book, so use them wisely! Have fun!

This project was made possible through the database generously created by Wikipedia and made available for public use by Jasminyas.
